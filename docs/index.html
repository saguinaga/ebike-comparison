Under 16 cannot operate Class 3 e-bike — A person under 16 years of age shall not operate a class 3 electric bicycle. Source
Under 18 helmet required — Riders under 18 must wear an ASTM or CPSC approved helmet on streets and bikeways. Source
Lights and reflectors required at night — Bicycles operated at night need a white front light, red rear reflector/light, and side reflectors. Source
Beach/park paths: 10 mph max — Beach bicycle path and city parks: 10 mph maximum; 5 mph when pedestrians present. Source
HBPD free e-bike training — Huntington Beach Police offer free youth e-bike safety training with parent participation. Source
