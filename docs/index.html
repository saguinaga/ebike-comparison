Under 16 cannot operate Class 3 e-bike

A person under 16 years of age shall not operate a class 3 electric bicycle. Source →

Under 18 helmet required

Riders under 18 must wear an ASTM or CPSC approved helmet on streets and bikeways. Source →

Lights and reflectors required at night

Bicycles operated at night need a white front light, red rear reflector/light, and side reflectors. Source →

Beach/park paths: 10 mph max
